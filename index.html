Our services

Service Description
Broadband Internet High-speed internet access delivered through DSL, cable, fiber-optic, or other technologies, allowing users to browse the web, stream media, download/upload files, and use online applications.
Wi-Fi Wireless local area network (WLAN) connectivity, enabling users to connect multiple devices (e.g., smartphones, tablets, laptops) to the internet without the need for physical cables.
Home Internet Plans Different packages with varying speeds and data caps to meet the needs of different households. Plans may include unlimited data, faster speeds for streaming/gaming, and options for multiple devices.
Business Internet Internet connectivity tailored for businesses, providing dedicated bandwidth, higher speeds, service level agreements (SLAs), and support for multiple users, VPN connections, online transactions, and other business-specific requirements.
